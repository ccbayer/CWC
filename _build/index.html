Our Story

Behind the Scenes

It starts deep within the rolling hills of Kentucky and perhaps even further back than that.

We believe it begins with the artisan farmers who hold a reverential respect for the livestock they raise. These beautiful beasts are of the never-an-antibiotic, straight-from-the-grassy-pasture, happy animals, variety, and the difference is evident in the taste and in the good vibes sealed into each package.

When these quality proteins make their way into our shop, we consider it our privilege to contribute to the story by handcrafting the most delicious and distinctive cured meats we possibly can. We’re intent to define New American Styles of spicing, seasoning and curing while drawing on time-tested, traditional recipes. And we never use ingredients you can’t pronounce. Instead of compound nitrates, you’ll find a combination of dehydrated celery juice and sea salt for curing our naturally dried and aged meats. Simple. Tasty. And all that’s needed.

Commonwealth Cure has landed. From our loving hands to your hungry bellies, made possible in glorious Louisville, Kentucky. Please enjoy!
